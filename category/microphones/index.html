What is the difference between UHF and VHF wireless microphones?
UHF (Ultra High Frequency) microphones operate at higher frequencies (300MHz–3GHz), offering better audio quality, less interference, and longer range than VHF mics. For professional use in India — especially at events with multiple mics — UHF is strongly recommended. All Punishka Trading wireless mics are UHF.
