Frequently Asked Questions

What is a network plate for speakers?
A network plate (passive crossover) divides the audio signal into frequency bands — sending lows to woofers, mids to mid-range drivers, and highs to tweeters. It protects drivers from damage and ensures balanced sound output from your PA or DJ speaker system.
Do you sell pressure/compression drivers for PA speakers?
