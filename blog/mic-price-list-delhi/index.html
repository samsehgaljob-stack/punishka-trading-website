What's Included in a Wireless Mic Set?

A standard wireless mic set includes: 1 or 2 microphone transmitters (handheld or bodypack), 1 receiver unit (rack-mount or tabletop), 1/4-inch or XLR output cable, rack mounting brackets (on some models), and carrying case on premium models. Always confirm what's included before purchasing, as pricing can vary significantly based on accessories.

UHF vs VHF — Which to Buy?

For any professional use — DJ events, weddings, corporate functions — always choose UHF wireless microphones. UHF operates in the 500–900MHz range and provides interference-free operation even in crowded venues where multiple wireless devices are in use.
