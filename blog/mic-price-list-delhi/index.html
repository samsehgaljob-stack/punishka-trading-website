Single Channel UHF Wireless Mic

One transmitter (handheld), one receiver. Ideal for solo DJs, small events, karaoke setups, and church or school functions.

  • Budget non-diversity single UHF: ₹2,200–₹4,500
  • Mid-range single UHF (diversity): ₹4,500–₹9,000
  • Professional single UHF (true diversity): ₹9,000–₹18,000

Dual Channel UHF Wireless Mic System

Two transmitters, one receiver with two independent channels. The most popular choice for DJ + anchor setups at Indian weddings and events.

  • Budget dual UHF (non-diversity): ₹4,000–₹7,500
  • Mid-range dual UHF (diversity): ₹8,000–₹16,000
  • Professional dual UHF (diversity, combo): ₹16,000–₹32,000

4-Channel UHF Wireless Mic System

Four simultaneous wireless channels from one receiver unit. Essential for large events with multiple performers, corporate galas, and multi-anchor stage shows.

  • 4-channel UHF diversity system: ₹18,000–₹38,000
  • Professional 4-channel (switchable freq): ₹35,000–₹65,000

Lavalier / Lapel Wireless Mic

Belt-pack transmitter with small clip-on lapel mic. Hands-free operation for event hosts, presenters, and performers.

  • Single UHF lapel system: ₹4,500–₹12,000
  • Dual UHF lapel + handheld combo: ₹12,000–₹28,000

Headset Wireless Mic

Headband-mounted mic with belt-pack transmitter. Used by anchors, fitness instructors, and performers who need both hands free and guaranteed mic positioning.

  • UHF headset wireless: ₹5,500–₹15,000

What Affects Wireless Mic Price?

  • Frequency technology: UHF costs more than VHF but performs far better in Delhi NCR's congested RF spectrum
  • Diversity: True diversity (two independent receiver paths) costs more but eliminates dropouts completely
  • Frequency switching: Models with adjustable frequencies cost more but are future-proof if interference occurs
  • Capsule quality: Better mic capsule = better voice clarity, especially for vocals in noisy event environments
