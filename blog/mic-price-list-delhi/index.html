Wireless Microphone Price List Delhi 2025

📅 22 April 2026
Englishहिंदी

If you're shopping for a wireless microphone in Delhi, prices swing wildly between shops — the same UHF dual-handheld set might cost ₹4,500 in one shop and ₹12,000 two doors down. This updated April 2025 price list from Rohini wholesale market will help you know what you should actually pay, what features to look for, and which models are worth your money.

Why Prices Vary So Much

Wireless mic pricing in Delhi depends on six factors:

  • Frequency band: UHF (higher quality) vs VHF (cheaper, more interference)
  • Number of channels: Fixed single frequency vs selectable 100+ channels
  • Receiver quality: True diversity (dual antenna) vs single-antenna
  • Battery type: AA batteries vs rechargeable
  • Build quality: Plastic casing vs metal housing
  • Brand: Chinese unbranded, Indian assembly, or international brands

VHF Wireless Microphones — Delhi Prices

Cheapest option, best for small indoor events and low-budget setups. Prone to interference from 2.4 GHz devices.

  • Single handheld VHF: ₹1,200 – ₹2,800
  • Dual handheld VHF: ₹2,400 – ₹4,800
  • VHF lapel/collar mic: ₹1,800 – ₹3,500
  • VHF headset mic: ₹2,200 – ₹4,200

UHF Wireless Microphones — Delhi Prices

Industry standard for weddings, events, and professional use. Cleaner signal, longer range (50–100 meters), fewer dropouts.

  • Single handheld UHF (fixed freq): ₹2,800 – ₹5,200
  • Dual handheld UHF (fixed freq): ₹4,500 – ₹9,500
  • Dual handheld UHF (selectable): ₹8,500 – ₹18,000
  • Quad UHF mic system: ₹14,000 – ₹28,000
  • UHF lapel mic (pro): ₹4,800 – ₹12,000
  • UHF headset mic: ₹5,200 – ₹14,000

Professional / International Brands

  • Shure BLX dual handheld: ₹38,000 – ₹55,000
