What is the PU-60XT PA Driver Unit?

A PA driver unit (also called a compression driver or horn driver) is the transducer that converts electrical audio signal into sound inside a horn speaker. The PU-60XT is a 60-watt rated compression driver designed for use with standard PA horns — the classic "voice of God" speakers used at temples, masjids, stadiums, construction sites, and outdoor events across India.

What is a 100V Line System?

A 100V line (constant voltage) PA system is the standard for distributed audio in India — used in schools, hospitals, factories, religious sites, and large campuses. Instead of running multiple low-impedance speakers from a single amp, a 100V line system uses a step-up transformer at the amplifier and step-down transformers at each speaker, allowing you to run dozens of speakers over long cable runs without signal loss. The PU-60XT is designed to plug directly into these systems.

How to Set Up the PU-60XT

  1. Mount the horn: Attach your PA horn to a pole or bracket at height (minimum 3 meters for coverage)
  2. Connect the driver: Thread the PU-60XT into the horn throat — standard thread fitting
  3. Wire to amplifier: For 100V line systems, connect via the horn's built-in transformer taps. For direct 8-ohm connection, run speaker cable directly
  4. Set amplifier gain: Start at 50% volume and increase gradually — PA drivers can distort if overdriven
  5. Test coverage: Walk the area and check for even sound distribution from the horn
