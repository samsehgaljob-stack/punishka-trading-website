How to Choose PA Speakers for DJ Setup — Complete India Guide

📅 20 April 2026
Englishहिंदी

A DJ rig is only as good as the PA speakers it pushes sound through. Amplifiers, mixers, and even the DJ himself don't matter if the speakers can't reproduce cleanly at loud SPL. Based on what we've shipped to event companies from our Rohini, Delhi warehouse, here's the framework we use to help buyers pick the right PA.

Step 1 — Understand What PA Speakers Actually Do

PA (Public Address) speakers are full-range cabinets designed for live sound. Unlike home-theatre speakers, PAs have high sensitivity (95–102 dB @ 1W/1m), ruggedized voice coils, and horn-loaded tweeters to project sound 20–50 metres without losing clarity. For Indian weddings, PA speakers must also survive dust, voltage spikes, and 8-hour continuous use.

Step 2 — Pick Driver Size

  • 10-inch top: Good for vocal monitors, small rooms up to 500 sq ft
  • 12-inch top: The workhorse — ideal for 500–1500 sq ft banquet halls
  • 15-inch top: Big output for 1500–3000 sq ft with punchier mids
  • 18-inch sub: Required for any wedding with bass-heavy Bollywood/punjabi music
  • Dual 18-inch sub or 21-inch: Large outdoor venues, farmhouse setups

Step 3 — Match Wattage Correctly

Always check the RMS (continuous) wattage, not PMPO. A good 12-inch top should have 300–500W RMS, a 15-inch top 500–800W RMS, and an 18-inch subwoofer 800–1500W RMS. Pair with an amplifier delivering ~2x the RMS (see our amplifier guide).

Step 4 — Sensitivity and SPL

Sensitivity tells you how loud a speaker gets per watt. 95 dB @ 1W/1m is average; 100 dB @ 1W/1m is excellent. A 100 dB sensitivity speaker is effectively twice as loud as a 97 dB speaker for the same power input. For Indian weddings where SPL needs to cut through 300 guests talking, prioritize sensitivity.

Step 5 — Dispersion Pattern

Standard PA tops have 90° x 60° dispersion (horizontal x vertical). For long narrow banquet halls, use 60° x 40° for longer throw. For wide open lawns, choose 90° x 60° or stack line-array modules. Getting dispersion wrong leaves dead zones where guests can't hear the DJ.

Step 6 — Passive vs Active

Passive PAs need an external amplifier — this is the Indian wedding standard, because you can pair with the high-headroom PU8000. Active (powered) PAs have a built-in amp and are simpler but more expensive and harder to repair. For rental fleets, always passive.

Final Checklist

Before you pay, verify: RMS wattage printed on the rear panel, driver brand (look for genuine Faital, B&C, P Audio, or Italian-design equivalents), cabinet build (18mm Birch ply > 15mm MDF), grille mesh thickness, and Speakon connectors (not banana plugs). PA Speaker क्या होते हैं?

PA यानि Public Address speakers live sound के लिए बने full-range cabinet हैं. Home theatre से इनमें बहुत फर्क है — इनकी sensitivity 95–102 dB होती है, voice coil रिज़्ड होती है, और horn-loaded tweeter 20–50 meter तक बिना loss के sound भेजता है. शादी में PA को dust, voltage spike और 8-hour continuous use भी झेलना पड़ता है.

Driver Size कैसे चुनें?

  • 10-inch: Vocal monitor, 500 sq ft तक
  • 12-inch: Workhorse — 500–1500 sq ft banquet hall
  • 15-inch: 1500–3000 sq ft, punchy mids
  • 18-inch sub: Bollywood/Punjabi bass के लिए must
  • Dual 18 या 21-inch: Outdoor farmhouse setup

Wattage सही Match कीजिए

हमेशा RMS देखिए, PMPO नहीं. 12-inch top = 300–500W RMS, 15-inch = 500–800W, 18-inch sub = 800–1500W RMS. Amplifier 2x RMS देना चाहिए — details हमारी Amplifier Guide में हैं.

Sensitivity और SPL

Sensitivity बताती है कि 1 watt पर speaker कितना loud होता है. 95 dB average है, 100 dB excellent है. 100 dB वाला speaker 97 dB वाले से दोगुना loud होता है. शादी में 300 लोगों की बातों के बीच sound cut करना है तो sensitivity ज़रूरी है.

Dispersion Pattern

Standard PA top 90° x 60° dispersion देते हैं. Narrow long hall के लिए 60° x 40° लें (ज्यादा throw). Wide lawn के लिए 90° x 60° या line array. गलत dispersion से dead zones बन जाती हैं जहाँ DJ sunai नहीं देता.

Passive vs Active

Passive PA को external amp चाहिए — Indian wedding का standard यही है, क्योंकि PU8000 जैसा high-headroom amp pair कर सकते हैं. Active PA में amp built-in होता है, simpler है पर महंगा और repair मुश्किल. Rental के लिए हमेशा passive लें.
