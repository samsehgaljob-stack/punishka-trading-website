Punishka 18-Inch Subwoofer — Serious Bass for Serious DJs

The Punishka 18-inch subwoofer delivers the deep, chest-pounding bass that Indian DJ events demand. Built on a high-power 18-inch ferrite driver in a tuned ported cabinet, it handles everything from wedding baraats to open-air concerts. Available wholesale from Punishka Trading, Rohini, New Delhi.

Specifications

  • Driver: 18-inch ferrite magnet woofer
  • Power: 1000W-1200W program
  • Sensitivity: 98-100 dB/W/m
  • Frequency Response: 35Hz-120Hz
  • Impedance: 4 or 8 ohm
  • Cabinet: Ported, tuned ~45Hz, 18mm plywood
  • Connectors: Speakon NL4 + binding post
  • Weight: 38-45 kg

Why It Performs at Indian Events

  • Ferrite magnet — reliable in extreme summer heat, no magnetic flux loss
  • ~45Hz port tuning — musical bass for Bollywood, bhangra and EDM
  • 1000W+ handling — push hard with PU8000 bridged (1600W) with headroom

Best Amplifier Pairing

The PU8000 from Punishka Trading in bridged mode (1600W into 8 ohm) is the ideal match. Always use a low-pass crossover set to 80-120Hz to protect the driver.

Placement Tips

  • Stack two units for major events — +3dB output
  • Keep at floor level — bass is omnidirectional
  • Stack PA tops on subs or on stands
  • For directional bass, use cardioid sub array (one reversed)
