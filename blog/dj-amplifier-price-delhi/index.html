DJ Amplifier Price List Delhi 2025 — Wholesale Rates

Delhi's Rohini market is one of India's biggest wholesale hubs for DJ amplifiers. Whether you need a budget 1000W amp for a small setup or a professional 8000W powerhouse for large weddings and concerts, this price list gives you a realistic view of what to expect in 2025.

Budget Amplifiers (1000W–2000W)

Entry-level DJ amplifiers in Delhi's wholesale market are ideal for DJs just starting out or for smaller indoor events. These units handle 50–200 person gatherings comfortably.

  • 1000W stereo amp (Class AB): ₹6,500–₹11,000
  • 1500W stereo amp: ₹9,000–₹14,000
  • 2000W stereo amp (Class H): ₹12,000–₹18,000

At this price range, look for built-in clip limiters and variable-speed cooling fans. Avoid units without thermal protection — Delhi's summer heat can push cheap amplifiers into thermal shutdown mid-event.

The sweet spot for professional mobile DJs in Delhi NCR. These amplifiers power most wedding, birthday, and corporate event setups with authority.

  • 3000W DJ amp: ₹18,000–₹26,000
  • 4000W DJ amp (PU7800 class): ₹24,000–₹36,000
  • 5000W DJ amp: ₹30,000–₹45,000

Professional Amplifiers (6000W–8000W+)

For large outdoor events, concert productions, and multi-stack PA systems in Delhi, Gurgaon, and Noida.

  • 6000W professional amp: ₹40,000–₹58,000
  • 8000W DJ amp (PU8000): ₹48,000–₹68,000
  • 10000W+ touring amp: ₹70,000–₹1,10,000

The PU8000 is the flagship choice for event companies in Delhi NCR. What Affects Amplifier Pricing?

  • Power class: Class-H and Class-D amps are pricier but more efficient than Class-AB
  • Protection features: Short-circuit, clip, thermal, and DC protection add to cost but save money long-term
  • Build quality: Toroidal transformers, military-grade capacitors, and heavy-gauge chassis cost more but last decades
  • Brand vs local: Branded amps cost more; Rohini market also stocks reliable locally assembled units at lower prices
