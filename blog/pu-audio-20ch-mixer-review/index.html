PU Audio 20CH Mixer DSP99 TGD20 — Professional Console

The PU Audio 20-Channel Mixer DSP99 TGD20 is a professional mixing console for medium to large live events in India. It combines 20 input channels with built-in 99 DSP effects and digital preamps. Available from Punishka Trading, Rohini, New Delhi at wholesale prices.

Specifications

  • Channels: 20 (16 mono XLR/TRS + 4 stereo line)
  • DSP Effects: 99 onboard (reverb, delay, chorus, flanger, pitch)
  • EQ: 4-band on mono, 3-band on stereo channels
  • Aux Sends: 4 aux sends for monitors and effects
  • Outputs: Main XLR stereo, sub out, control room out, headphone
  • USB: Playback and recording
  • Phantom Power: +48V switchable per channel group
  • Meters: LED bargraph master meters

DSP99 Effects Processor

99 professional effect presets including concert hall reverb, echo, chorus, flanger, and pitch correction. Front panel encoder lets you recall effects instantly during a live show. Most popular for DJs: hall reverb for vocals, echo for transitions.

Who Should Use the TGD20?

  • DJs doing 200-600 person events
  • Small concerts needing multiple monitor mixes
  • Wedding setups with multiple mics and music sources
  • Events needing USB playback + live mixing together

TGD20 vs MG-P32X

TGD20 is ideal for events with up to 20 sources. MG-P32X offers 32 channels for larger productions. Both available from Punishka Trading.

Setup Tips

  1. Channels 1-8 for mics and instruments
  2. Channels 17-20 for USB playback
  3. Assign DSP effects on aux send/return loop
  4. Use aux sends 3-4 for stage monitors
