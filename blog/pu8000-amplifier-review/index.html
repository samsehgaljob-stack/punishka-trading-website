The PU8000 — Punishka Trading's Flagship Power Amplifier

The Punishka PU8000 1600W Power Amplifier is one of the most trusted professional amplifiers used by DJs and PA system operators across India. Built for the demanding conditions of Indian live events — from hot outdoor summer weddings to high-energy college concerts — the PU8000 delivers consistent, reliable power with built-in protection features that keep your system safe. Available exclusively at Punishka Trading, Rohini, New Delhi.

PU8000 Technical Specifications

  • Output Power (Stereo @ 4Ω): 800W per channel
  • Output Power (Stereo @ 8Ω): 500W per channel
  • Output Power (Bridged @ 8Ω): 1600W
  • Frequency Response: 20Hz–20kHz (±1dB)
  • THD: <0.1% at rated power
  • Input Sensitivity: 0.775V / 1V switchable
  • Input Impedance: 20kΩ balanced
  • S/N Ratio: >105dB
  • Cooling: Variable speed front-to-rear fan cooling
  • Protection: DC, short circuit, thermal, overload
  • Dimensions: 2U rack mount

Build Quality and Design

The PU8000 is built in a robust 2U rack-mount chassis with front-to-rear forced air cooling. The variable-speed fan runs quietly at low loads and ramps up intelligently when the amplifier works harder — essential for long DJ sets in hot Indian climates. The front panel features level controls, clip indicators, signal LEDs, and a bridge/stereo mode switch. The rear panel has balanced XLR inputs and Speakon/binding post speaker outputs for professional connectivity.

Sound Quality

In our testing at real events in Delhi-NCR, the PU8000 delivers tight, punchy bass when driving 18-inch ferrite subwoofers and clean, dynamic tops when powering full-range PA cabinets. The low distortion figure (<0.1% THD) translates to a clean, transparent sound that lets your music speak without coloration. The high signal-to-noise ratio (>105dB) means a quiet noise floor even in sensitive speaker setups.

Best Use Cases for the PU8000

  • Driving 18-inch or 21-inch subwoofer cabinets in bridged mode (1600W into 8Ω)
  • Powering full-range 15-inch PA tops in stereo mode
  • Main amplifier for wedding and event DJ setups of 300–800 people
  • Bi-amp setups with one PU8000 per zone (tops and subs separately)

PU8000 vs PU7800 — Which to Choose?

The PU8000 offers higher output power and improved thermal management over the PU7800. For events requiring maximum headroom and reliable long-session performance, the PU8000 is the clear choice. The PU7800 remains excellent for medium events and budget-conscious setups.
