PBT 601 PA Amplifier — Professional Grade Power for Indian PA Systems

The PBT 601 PA Amplifier is a professional-grade power amplifier purpose-built for Public Address (PA) applications in India. Whether you're running a speech system for a corporate event, a multi-zone background music system for a mall, or a high-output sound reinforcement rig for a political rally, the PBT 601 handles the load.

Specifications at a Glance

  • Rated Output Power: 600W × 2 at 4Ω (stereo); 1200W at 8Ω (bridged mono)
  • Circuit Topology: Class-H with MOSFET driver stage
  • Frequency Response: 20Hz – 20kHz (±0.5dB)
  • THD+N: <0.03% at 1kHz
  • Signal-to-Noise Ratio: >108dB (A-weighted)
  • Input: Balanced XLR and TRS combo jack
  • Output: Speakon NL4 + binding posts
  • Protection: Clip, DC, thermal, in-rush, and RF interference rejection
  • Dimensions: 2U rack-mount; 482mm × 88mm × 350mm
  • Weight: Approx. 8.5 kg

Why the PBT 601 Stands Out in PA Applications

Unlike typical DJ amplifiers, the PBT 601 is optimized for speech intelligibility. Its flat frequency response and ultra-low THD+N (<0.03%) preserve the natural characteristics of voice — critical in corporate AV, places of worship, educational institutions, and government venues. The SNR of 108dB means background noise is virtually absent even at high gain settings.
