M6 USB Mixer — The Compact Powerhouse for Indian DJs

The M6 USB Mixer is a compact, feature-packed mixing console popular among small to medium event DJs, home studio users, and content creators across India. Available from Punishka Trading in Rohini, Delhi, it offers professional-grade features at an accessible price point.

M6 USB Mixer Specifications

  • Channels: 6-channel (4 mono XLR/TRS combo + 2 stereo RCA)
  • USB Interface: Built-in 2-channel USB audio interface
  • EQ: 3-band EQ per channel
  • Effects: Built-in digital reverb and echo
  • Outputs: Main XLR out, monitor out, headphone out
  • Phantom Power: +48V for condenser mics
  • USB: Plug-and-play, no driver needed

Who Is the M6 For?

  • Small indoor events, birthday parties, kitty parties
  • Home studio recording via USB
  • YouTubers and content creators needing a simple audio interface
  • Corporate AV setups needing compact mixer with recording
  • Backup mixer for professional DJs

Setting Up the M6

  1. Connect mics/instruments to channels 1-4 via XLR or TRS
  2. Connect RCA sources to channels 5-6
  3. Set gain on each channel, faders to unity
  4. Connect main XLR output to your amplifier
  5. For USB recording: connect to PC, appears as standard audio device

M6 Pros and Limitations

Pros: Very compact, USB recording built-in, phantom power, affordable, simple to operate. Limitations: Only 6 channels — not for large multi-source events. For more channels consider KMC8 or MG-P32X from Punishka Trading.
