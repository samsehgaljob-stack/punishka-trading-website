PU8000 vs PU7800 Amplifier — Which One Should You Buy?

Two of the most popular professional DJ amplifiers in Delhi's Rohini wholesale market are the PU8000 and PU7800. Both are Class-H designs with serious power output, but they serve different event scales. This detailed comparison helps you pick the right one for your budget and requirements.

PU7800 — The Professional Mid-Range Workhorse

The PU7800 is a 2-channel Class-H amplifier delivering approximately 3800W–4000W per channel at 4Ω (bridge mode: up to 7600W). It's the most popular amplifier among professional mobile DJs in Delhi NCR doing weddings, corporate events, and medium-to-large parties.

Key Specs

  • Output power: ~4000W per channel @ 4Ω
  • Bridge output: ~7600W @ 8Ω
  • Impedance: 4Ω / 8Ω stereo, 8Ω bridged
  • THD: <0.1% at rated power
  • Cooling: Variable-speed forced-air fans
  • Protection: Clip, thermal, short-circuit, DC offset
  • Weight: ~18–22 kg
  • Delhi wholesale price: ₹24,000–₹36,000

Best for: Events of 200–600 people. Two PU7800 units (one for tops, one for subs) handle most professional Indian wedding setups perfectly.

PU8000 — The Flagship Powerhouse

The PU8000 is the step-up flagship, delivering approximately 4500W–5000W per channel at 4Ω (bridge: up to 9000W+). It's built for large-scale event production — outdoor concerts, large wedding grounds, and multi-stack PA systems across Delhi NCR.

Key Specs

  • Output power: ~5000W per channel @ 4Ω
  • Bridge output: ~9000W+ @ 8Ω
  • Impedance: 4Ω / 8Ω stereo, 8Ω bridged
  • THD: <0.08% at rated power
  • Cooling: Dual variable-speed fan arrays
  • Protection: Full suite + soft-start for inrush control
  • Weight: ~24–28 kg
  • Delhi wholesale price: ₹38,000–₹55,000

Best for: Large events of 400–1000+ people, outdoor productions, professional event companies needing maximum headroom.

Head-to-Head Comparison

  • Power: PU8000 wins — ~25% more output per channel
  • Price: PU7800 wins — typically ₹12,000–₹20,000 cheaper
  • Weight / portability: PU7800 wins — lighter, easier to carry
  • Headroom: PU8000 wins — never clips on large outdoor stages
  • Value for typical Indian DJ: PU7800 wins — overkill to pay for PU8000 if events are under 500 people

Which Should You Buy?

Buy the PU7800 if you're a professional mobile DJ handling weddings and events up to 500 people — it's the best value for money in this segment.

Buy the PU8000 if you run a full event production company, regularly do large outdoor concerts or farm house events in Delhi NCR, and need maximum headroom without ever clipping. Visit Punishka Trading, Rohini to demo both units.
