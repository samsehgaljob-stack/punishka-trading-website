Frequently Asked Questions

What is a network plate crossover used for?
A network plate crossover splits the audio signal from your amplifier into separate frequency bands — typically bass (for woofer) and treble (for tweeter). This prevents the tweeter from receiving damaging bass frequencies and ensures clean sound reproduction.
