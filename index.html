Our Story

About Punishka Trading

India's most trusted professional DJ equipment supplier since 2008. Direct importers of world-class audio gear.

Who We Are

Punishka Trading was founded with one clear mission: bring world-class professional DJ equipment to India at prices that make sense.

We are direct importers and authorized distributors of professional DJ mixers, speakers, amplifiers, controllers, and lighting equipment. By eliminating middlemen, we pass on massive savings directly to DJs, event companies, clubs, and sound rental businesses across India.

Over 15 years in the industry and 1,000+ satisfied customers from Kashmir to Kanyakumari — we know what Indian professionals need and we deliver it.

Our Promise

Every product we sell comes with a 100% authenticity guarantee. No grey market. No duplicates. No compromise.

We provide GST invoices on all purchases, making us the preferred supplier for businesses, event companies, and professionals who need proper documentation.

Our expert team is available 6 days a week to help you choose the right gear — whether you're setting up a home studio, a 5000-person event venue, or a chain of nightclubs.
