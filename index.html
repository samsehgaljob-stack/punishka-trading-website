Who We Are

Punishka Trading is your one-stop destination for professional DJ amplifiers, mixers, microphones, speakers, network plates, and accessories. Based in Rohini Sector 17, New Delhi, we have been serving DJs, event companies, sound rental businesses, and clubs across India for over 8 years.

We carry top-performing products like the PU8000 & PU7800 Class-H Amplifiers, heavy-duty mixers (GB4 24-channel, GB8 32-channel), professional microphones, pure copper network plates, pressure mid drivers, and much more. All products are 100% original, tested, and GST invoiced.
