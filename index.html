Our Story

About Punishka Trading

India's most trusted DJ equipment supplier in New Delhi since 2018.

Who We Are

Punishka Trading is your one-stop destination for professional DJ amplifiers, mixers, microphones, speakers, network plates, and accessories. Based in Rohini Sector 17, New Delhi, we have been serving DJs, event companies, sound rental businesses, and clubs across India for over 8 years.

We carry top-performing products like the PU8000 & PU7800 Class-H Amplifiers, heavy-duty mixers (GB4 24-channel, GB8 32-channel), professional microphones, pure copper network plates, pressure mid drivers, and much more. All products are 100% original, tested, and GST invoiced.

✓ GST Registered | GSTIN: Verified Business

Our Promise

We believe every DJ and sound professional deserves access to world-class equipment at fair prices. That's why we offer the most competitive wholesale pricing in Delhi, with live meter testing available in store so you see real output before buying.

Our team is available 6 days a week for free consultation. Not sure what amplifier suits your setup? Which mixer to choose for your venue? Just call or WhatsApp us — we'll help you pick the right gear for your budget and requirements.
